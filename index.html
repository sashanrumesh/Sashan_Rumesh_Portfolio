Design and Analysis
Savonius Wind Turbines

The Savonius Vertical Axis Wind Turbine (VAWT) with PVC blades is a simple, cost-effective, and sustainable way to generate small-scale renewable energy. This project focused on improving the turbine’s design by carefully selecting materials, optimizing blade shape, and ensuring strong structural support. As a result, the turbine achieved an efficiency of 16.32% at a wind speed of 5 m/s, proving its ability to generate power even in low-wind conditions.

3D Design

Plastic Injection Machine 3D Design

This project showcases a complete 3D CAD model of a plastic injection molding machine. Designed in SolidWorks, the assembly includes a hopper, rotating screw, motor system, heating chamber, and mold section. The machine is built for efficient plastic molding processes, ideal for small to medium-scale manufacturing setups.

Energy Research

Solar-Wind Hybrid Microgrid System

This project presents a Solar-Wind Hybrid Microgrid System with battery storage, designed to power 20 rural households in Sri Lanka. It combines renewable energy sources to ensure reliable, sustainable, and cost-effective electrification, improving living standards while promoting clean energy adoption in remote areas.
